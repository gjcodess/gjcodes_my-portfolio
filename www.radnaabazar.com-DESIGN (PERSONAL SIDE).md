# Design System Inspired by Radnaabazar

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Radnaabazar embodies a sophisticated, minimalist aesthetic rooted in dark mode with vibrant neon accents. The design evokes a sense of contemporary tech-forward elegance paired with human warmth—a personal portfolio that balances professional presentation with intimate storytelling. Monospace typography (JetBrains Mono) reinforces a technical, deliberate character, while the generous use of whitespace and carefully controlled contrast creates an environment of calm focus. The occasional pop of electric cyan (`#00FF99`) against deep navy backgrounds introduces dynamic energy, suggesting innovation and personality beneath a refined surface.

**Key Characteristics**
- Dark-first design philosophy with high contrast readability
- Neon accent colors providing visual hierarchy and emotional resonance
- Monospace typography throughout for consistency and technical credibility
- Generous whitespace and breathing room between content blocks
- Minimal use of shadows; clean, flat aesthetic
- Motion-ready color system supporting active, hover, and focus states

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **Primary Action** (`#00FF99`): Vibrant cyan-green used for call-to-action buttons, active navigation states, and key interactive elements that demand immediate attention.
- **Primary Background** (`#0F0E1A`): Deep navy-black providing the foundation for dark mode layouts and primary content areas.

### Accent Colors
- **Purple Accent** (`#A855F7`): Secondary action color and emphasis element for alternative interactive states.
- **Blue Accent** (`#3B82F6`): Tertiary emphasis color for supplementary interactive elements and secondary CTAs.
- **Sky Blue** (`#60A5FA`): Light blue for subtle highlights and supplementary visual accents.

### Interactive
- **Link Default** (`#FFFFFF`): White text for standard navigation and body links on dark backgrounds.
- **Link Active** (`#00FF99`): Cyan-green underline and text color for active/focused links.
- **Button Ghost** (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.95)`): Nearly opaque white for secondary button states.

### Neutral Scale
- **Text Primary** (`#FFFFFF`): Primary readable text on dark backgrounds.
- **Text Secondary** (`#E5E7EB`): Light gray for secondary text, body copy, and reduced-emphasis content.
- **Text Tertiary** (`#374151`): Medium gray for captions, metadata, and disabled states.
- **Text Muted** (`#525252`): Darker gray for inactive or placeholder text.
- **Text Disabled** (`#404040`): Very dark gray for disabled UI elements.

### Surface & Borders
- **Card Border** (`#D4D4D4`): Light gray border for cards and contained elements on dark backgrounds.
- **Divider** (`#262626`): Dark gray divider between sections.
- **Overlay Dark** (`#000000`): Pure black for deep overlay effects.

### Semantic / Status
- **Error** (`#EF4444`): Red for error messages, validation failures, and dangerous actions.
- **Success** (`#22C55E`): Green for successful operations, completed actions, and affirmative feedback.
- **Warning** (`#EAB308`): Amber for cautionary messages and warning states.
